Legal LinQ collaborates with universities and international organisations towards a decision tool for dispute resolution.

Central question is: 'What is the best method for a given dispute'?

Currently these are prototypes only and hence do NOT provide any legal advice.

DRP latest Follow-up app, now with fingerprint, work in progress, subject to change. UNCITRAL 2017 First prototype as presented to the UNCITRAL at their 50th Session, Vienna 2017.
